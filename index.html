Badan Pengurus Harian (BPH) is the structural work tool of MBEC, it consist of:

  • President:

    • Fostering a dynamic and supportive environment that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and public speaking skills.

  • Vice President 1:

    • Held responsibility for division 1 which is Speech, Storytelling, Scrabble, Spelling bee, Newscast, and Debate.

  • Vice President 2:

Secretary

The administrative professionals who plays an integral role in MBEC.

The one who responsible of money and the process of obtaining funds to support the MBEC's operations.
